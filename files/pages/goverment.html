SALMON SPRINGS ADMINISTRATION

  • CapSquish

    Salmon Springs district court judge

    In 2024, CapSquish was elected as into the Salmon Springs District Court for his significant contributions in the consitutional drafting process. Having been a member of FishCultCraft since Season Five, CapSquish is affectionately known as the "local unc" and looks forward to working diligently to ensure a better Salmon Springs.

  • Donkey Kong

    Salmon Springs district court judge

    District Judge DonkeyKong, a member since Season Seven, is the distant cousin of the late CR1SI and proud uncle to Diddy Kong. He is the first member to serve in two separate government positions, and has an extensive public service record in the Salmon Springs Office of City Planning.

  • Hellmuth

    Salmon Springs district court judge

    As one of the newest faces to grace FishCultCraft, Hellmuth was elected into the Salmon Springs District Court armed with his friendly demeanor. He is the first elected official in FCC that has joined in a 2-digit numbered season, and is the partner of local face Apreil.
